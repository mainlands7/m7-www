Assignment of Declaration of Restrictions in the Mainlands of Tamarac Lakes Seventh Section

THIS ASSIGNMENT, made in Broward County, Florida, this 9th day of February, 1981 by and between:

LEADERSHIP HOUSING, INC., a Delaware corporation formerly known as Behring Properties, Inc., hereinafter Assignor

and

MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORPORATION, INC., a Florida corporation not-for-profit, hereinafter Assignee.

WHEREAS, Assignor reserved to itself certain rights under that certain Declaration of Restrictions dated the 31st day of March, 1969, and recorded April 2, 1969, in Broward County, Florida Official Records Book 3896, at Page 185; and

WHEREAS, said Declaration of Restrictions contemplates that Assignor may assign the rights, privileges, duties and responsibilities of the reservations and servitudes contained in said Declaration; and

WHEREAS, Assignor desires to effect an assignment of said Declaration, therefore

W I T N E S S E T H:

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the Assignment and the acceptance of same and the sum of One Dollar ($1.00) and other good and valuable considerations, Assignor hereby assigns, conveys, sells and transfers unto the Assignee all of its rights, duties and obligations, reservations and servitudes as contained in the Declaration of Restrictions dated the 31st day of March, 1969, and recorded on the 2nd day of April, 1969, in Official Records Book 3896, at Page 185, of the Public Records of Broward County, Florida, as amended pursuant to that certain Amended Order of the Broward Circuit Court dated January 12, 1979, as recorded in Official Records Book 7986, Page 741 of the Public Records of Broward County, Florida, which Declaration of Restrictions relates to the following described lands:

THE MAINLANDS OF TAMARAC LAKES SEVENTH SECTION, according to the plat thereof recorded in Plat Book 68, page 4, Public Records of Broward County, Florida.

The Assignment created hereby is acknowledged by the Assignor and the Assignee to include, but not be limited to the right to collect monies for reasonable costs of maintaining the various properties set forth in the aforesaid Declaration of Restrictions, the right to perform said maintenance functions, and the lien rights as set forth in the aforesaid Declaration of Restrictions.

Assignor deems it in the best interests of the residents of Mainlands Seven to assign the aforesaid servitudes and restrictions and the Assignee deems it in the best interests of the Corporation and its individual members to accept said assignment and all of the rights, duties, obligations and servitudes created thereby.

The Assignor and the Assignee further agree and acknowledge that Assignor shall hereafter be fully discharged from any and all obligations, rights and duties created by said Declaration of Restrictions and that said Assignment is conveyed and granted without recourse to the Assignor.

Dated this 9th day of February, 1981.

LEADERSHIP HOUSING, INC.

Acceptance of Assignment

In consideration of the foregoing Assignment and other good and valuable considerations, the receipt and adequacy of which are hereby acknowledged, the MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORP., INC. hereby accepts all of the terms and conditions of said Assignment and further acknowledges that it is fully aware of the terms, conditions and covenants of the Declaration of Restrictions assigned by the foregoing instrument.

The MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORP., INC., as Assignee, further acknowledges and recognizes that any lien rights created by the aforesaid Declaration of Restrictions assigned hereby to said Corporation shall at all times be inferior and subordinate to any lien rights created by any mortgage of record and existing upon the date of this Assignment or placed of record subsequent thereto, which mortgages may encumber any of the real properties subject to the aforesaid Declaration of Restrictions.
