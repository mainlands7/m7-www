Assignment of Declaration of Restrictions

WHEREAS, the City of Tamarac, P.O. Box 25010, Tamarac, Florida, a municipal corporation of the State of Florida, was the Assignee of certain obligations and servitudes pursuant to an Assignment of said restrictions, obligations and servitudes from BEHRING PROPERTIES, INC., a Florida corporation under an Assignment dated the 16th day of June, 1967, and

WHEREAS, the City of Tamarac, Florida, is desirous of assigning all of the rights, duties, obligations and servitudes as contained in that certain Declaration of Restrictions dated the 31st day of March, 1969, and recorded on the 2nd day of April, 1969, in the Official Records Book 3896, at Page 185, of the Public Records of Broward County, Florida, and that the City deems it in the best interests of the public to assign said restrictions, and

WHEREAS, the MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORPORATION, INC., 4914 N.W. 57 Street, Tamarac, Florida, is desirous of accepting said Assignment and assuming the rights, duties and obligations as set forth in said Declaration of Restrictions.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the Assignment and the acceptance of same and the sum of One Dollar ($1.00) and other good and valuable considerations, the City of Tamarac, Florida, hereby assigns, conveys, sells and transfers unto the MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORPORATION, INC., all of its rights, duties, obligations and servitudes as contained in the Declaration of Restrictions dated the 31st day of March, 1969, and recorded on the 2nd day of April, 1969, in the Official Records Book 3896, at Page 185, of the Public Records of Broward County, Florida. which Declarations of Restrictions relate to the following described lands:

THE MAINLANDS OF TAMARAC LAKES SEVENTH SECTION, according to the plat thereof recorded in Plat Book 68, Page 4, Public Records of Broward County, Florida.

That the Assignment created hereby is acknowledged by the Assignor and the Assignee to include, but not be limited to the right to collect monies for the reasonable costs of maintaining the various properties as set forth in the aforesaid Declaration of Restrictions, the right to perform said maintenance functions, and the lien rights as set forth in the aforesaid Declaration of Restrictions.

The City of Tamarac, Florida as the Assignor, deems it in the best interests of the general public to assign the aforesaid servitudes and restrictions and the MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORPORATION, INC., as the Assignee, deems it in the best interests of the Corporation and its individual members to accept said assignment and all of the rights, duties, obligations and servitudes created thereby.

The Assignor and the Assignee further agree and acknowledge that the City of Tamarac, as Assignor, shall hereafter be fully discharged from any and all obligations, rights and duties created by said Declaration of Restrictions and that said Assignment is conveyed and granted without recourse to the Assignor.

The Assignment shall take effect on the 25th day of April, 1980.

Acceptance of Assignment

In consideration of the foregoing Assignment and other good and valuable considerations, the receipt and adequacy of which are hereby acknowledged, the MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORPORATION, INC., hereby accepts all of the terms and conditions of said Assignment and further acknowledges that it is fully aware of the terms, conditions and covenants of the original Declaration of Restrictions assigned by the foregoing instrument.

The MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORPORATION, INC., as Assignee, further acknowledges and recognizes that any lien rights created by the aforesaid Declaration of Restrictions assigned hereby to said Corporation shall at all times be inferior and subordinate to any lien rights create by any mortgage of record existing upon the date of this Assignment or place of record subsequent thereto, which mortgage encumbers any of the real properties included in the aforesaid Declaration of Restrictions.

Resolution No. R-80-106

A Resolution Authorizing the Assignment of Certain Restrictions and Servitudes to Mainlands Seven Maintenance Corporation, Incorporated

WHEREAS, the City of Tamarac by Resolution No. 37-69 dated June 16, 1969 accepted an assignment from Behring Corporation, Inc. of certain restrictions and servitudes of lands in Mainlands Section 7; and

WHEREAS, the Mainlands Seven Maintenance Corporation, Inc., a Homeowners Association, has requested the City to assign said restrictions and servitudes to the corporation and have agreed to accept the same, and

WHEREAS, after investigation it does not appear that the City would be adversely affected by making the requested assignment.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF TAMARAC, FLORIDA:

SECTION 1: That the City Council hereby assigns to the Mainlands Seven Maintenance Corporation, Inc., any rights, duties, obligations or servitudes contained in paragraph #5 of the Declaration of Restrictions, and

SECTION 2: That the appropriate officials of the City of Tamarac are hereby authorized to execute the assignment on behalf of the City.

PASSED, APPROVED and ADOPTED THIS 23rd day of April, 1980.
