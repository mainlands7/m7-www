Mainlands Seven Maintenance Corporation

Corporate Resolution

WHEREAS, THE HOMEOWNERS OF MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORPORATION AT A GENERAL MEETING HELD ON NOVEMBER 20, 1992 AT THE MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORPORATION CLUBHOUSE AT WHICH 2/3 OF A QUORUM OF THE MEMBERS PRESENT, IN PERSON OR BY ABSENTEE BALLOT, VOTED TO AMEND THEIR BY-LAWS TO MODIFY THEIR DEED RESTRICTIONS TO CONFORM WITH THE FAIR HOUSING ACT OF 1988, AND WHEREAS IT IS THE DESIRE OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF MAINLANDS SEVEN MAINTENANCE CORP., INC. TO ADOPT AND RECORD THE AMENDMENT TO ITS BY-LAWS PURSUANT TO ARTICLE VI SECTION 2 OR ITS PRESENT BY-LAWS.

Dated: 12/21/92
